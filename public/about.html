About Retromatch

Retromatch is a modern matchmaking platform built specifically for Counter-Strike 1.6, one of the most iconic competitive shooters in gaming history. Our mission is to bring back the classic CS 1.6 experience — with the structure, balance, and reliability of modern matchmaking — all while staying true to the spirit of the original game.

Whether you're reliving your youth or discovering CS 1.6 for the first time, Retromatch offers a competitive, cheat-resistant, and community-focused environment where every round counts.

Why Retromatch?
  • Automatically create balanced 5v5 matches with skill-based ELO rating.
  • Provide server-side anti-cheat, match history, and player profiles.
  • Use a captain-based map veto system just like in the golden days.
  • Support broad compatibility across Windows, Linux, macOS, Android, and more.
  • Be accessible worldwide, especially where CS 1.6 is still thriving.
Community-Powered, Not Corporate

Retromatch is built by passionate CS 1.6 players for the community. We're self-hosted, lightweight, and sustainable. Our goal is long-term availability, not quick profit.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

🔫 What is Retromatch?

Retromatch is a matchmaking platform for Counter-Strike 1.6. It pairs players into 5v5 competitive matches using ELO-based ranking, with map vetoes and fast server creation.

🌎 Who is Retromatch for?

Anyone who loves CS 1.6 — from veterans to new players. We support both casuals and competitive players worldwide.

💵 Is Retromatch free?

Yes! Playing is free. Optional VIP subscriptions offer perks like reserved slots and badges.

🛡️ Do you support anti-cheat?

Yes. We use server-side protections and match review tools to detect and ban cheaters.

🗺️ How does map selection work?

We use a captain-based ban system. Each team bans maps until one remains, which is then loaded on a clean server.

⚙️ What platforms are supported?

We support Windows, Linux, macOS (via Wine), Android (via Xash3D), and even FreeBSD. If you can run CS 1.6, you can play.

🧩 Do you support mods or custom servers?

Our matchmaking servers are vanilla, but a "Rent Your Own Server" feature is coming soon for custom setups.

👮‍♂️ Can I become an admin?

Yes — limited paid admin roles are available. We also appoint unpaid moderators based on merit.
